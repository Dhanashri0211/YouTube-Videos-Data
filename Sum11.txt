Accenture is a global business strategy and consulting company . Of the Fortune Global 100 companies, the 100 biggest companies in the world, Accenture consults with 95 of them . As more countries enter into developed and industrialized markets, the company seems to be well positioned to help growing companies in so many countries . Despite recent ups and downs, financial analysts are optimistic about Accenture . The company offers just over a 1.5 dividend yield and trades for a reasonable 24.5 PE ratio . With innovators around each corner and a reasonable price tag to boot, value investors will find something to like about the company .
